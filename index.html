Verification of Fairness

Match Pro uses an advanced cryptographic shuffle to ensure 100% unbiased results.

  • Zone Isolation: Teammates are automatically distributed across different zones. No member of a team can fish the same zone as a teammate.
  • Fair Variety: The "Mandatory Rotation" engine ensures every angler is assigned a completely different zone on Day 2.
  • Fisher-Yates Algorithm: Pegs are assigned using a true random shuffle, eliminating human error or "sticky ticket" bias.
  • Transparency: All assignments are audit-ready and grouped by team for easy verification on the board.
