How to Use Match Pro

  • 1. Initial Setup: Enter the number of expected teams/indivs and click "Generate Entry Grid".
  • 2. Manual Additions: Use the "Add Team" or "Add Individual" buttons at the bottom for late arrivals.
  • 3. Draw: Click "Process Draw". This locks existing anglers to their pegs.
  • 4. Amendments: Click "Amend List". Add your new anglers. When you re-draw, existing anglers will not move; new ones will fill the gaps.
